Coaching & Pricing

Two Ways to Train With Me

Both plans are built on the same foundation: training designed around your physiology, your goals and the week you actually have. The difference is who steers it once you're underway.

Each plan is built to elicit a cardiovascular adaptation, written by someone who has spent a fifteen-year career in cardiac physiology.

We're coaches, not clinicians — we don't diagnose and we don't treat. But when something in your data looks unusual, we know when it may warrant a conversation with your GP rather than another training adjustment.

Common Questions

Good to Know

What sports do you coach?

My experience covers a range of sports requiring a strong aerobic base — I coach athletes across cycling, running, triathlon, tennis, football and general fitness.

How many hours a week do I need?

Plans are built around your real schedule, not the other way around. I'll talk you through what's realistic for you.

Is there a lock-in contract?

No. Billed month to month, cancel any time. No setup fee, either.

Do you coach remotely?

Yes. Coaching is delivered online through TrainingPeaks and Strava, so I can coach you wherever you're based.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, any time, in either direction. Same coach, same data, no restart.

What makes your coaching different?

Most coaches learn the cardiovascular system from the sports science literature, while I've spent a career inside it. That shapes how I read your data, how I build your cardiovascular base, and how I judge whether you're adapting or just accumulating physiological debt.
